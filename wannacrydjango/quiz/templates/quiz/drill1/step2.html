What does this mean?

Taking inventory, or making a list of what you have, is the first step in protecting your network.

Like with anything else, you need to know it exists if you want to protect it. Or in the timeless words of GI Joe-

“Knowing is half the battle”.

In this first drill, you will get a sense for the state of your network inventory. Start by guesstimating how many workstations are in your network.

What counts as a workstation?

Your workstation inventory guesstimate should include PCs, macs, laptops, and macbooks.

One of the challenges in managing your vulnerabilities is patching holes in company workstations that are outside of the office- include these in your inventory count as well.

Don't include your servers, firewalls, smartphones, tablets, printers, smart fridges, or the mess you have in AWS.
