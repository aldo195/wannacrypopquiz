Basic Workstation Inventory (Step 2/4)

Most organizations perform vulnerability scans at least once a year that sweep the network for computers and inspect them for obvious security holes. These vulnerability scans will serve as the foundation of your inventory.

Grab a copy of a vulnerability scan report for the inside of your network from the last year. If you don’t have a PDF version on your computer, you may need to access your scanner console or your scanning vendor’s website. You can also ask your IT team or Managed Service Provider to send you a copy of a recent network vulnerability assessment.
