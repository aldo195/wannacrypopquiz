{% if drill.reason == "no network monitoring" %}

Why?

By not having network monitoring in place at all, you are relying on a “prevention only” approach to Information Security. This approach is very risky because a significant amount of malware and hacking tools will bypass your antivirus, no matter how “next generation” it is.

What's next?

You need to have a shot at spotting breaches in your security between the time the malware gets passed your antivirus and the time the FBI notifies you that many of your customers are having their identities stolen and you’re to blame. Luckily, there some easy ways to get started with basic network monitoring. Start by setting up a dedicated email distribution list that will receive email notifications of threat detections. Then ask your firewall vendor for help sending high priority detections to that email distribution list, or consider signing up for OpenDNS and set their detections to go to your notification distribution list.
