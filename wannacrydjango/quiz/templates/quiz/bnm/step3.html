What does this mean?

While the WannaCry ransomware made headlines by bringing down key networks used by the UK health system, FedEx, and others, it only used two of the NSA cyberweapons released by The Shadow Brokers. The EternalRocks malware that came afterwards targets seven(!) vulnerabilities and creates the DoublePulsar backdoor vulnerability in the computers it infects.

How many of my hosts are affected?

Check for the following words in your VA report using whatever filter/search capability you have. In most document viewers, CTRL+F will suffice.

  • DoublePulsar
  • 99439 (In Tenable Nessus Reports)
  • QUID 70077 (In Qualys Reports)

For each match, you should be able to see the IP address or hostname. Each unique IP or hostname counts as one affected host.
